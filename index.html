Winston Churchill

British Prime Minister | 1847 - 1964

Sir Winston Churchill was a British writer, military leader and statesman. Twice named prime minister of the United Kingdom, he forged alliances with the United States and Soviet Union to defeat Nazi Germany during World War II.
